Username Requirements:

  • Must be 8 to 20 characters long.
  • Must start and end with a letter.

Password Requirements:

  • Must have a minimum length of 8 characters.
  • Must include 3 out of 4 of the following special requirements:
  1. Contain at least 1 upper-case letter.
  2. Contain at least 1 lower-case letter.
  3. Contain at least 1 special character (&, *, $).
  4. Contain at least 1 numeric character.
  • Cannot contain all or part of your username(Two or more consecutive characters from the user name cannot be used in the password).
